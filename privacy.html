Privacy Policy

Website Trust Checker respects user privacy. This page explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

When you use Website Trust Checker, we may process the website domain you submit for analysis. No personal information such as names, email addresses, or IP addresses is intentionally collected.

How Information Is Used

Submitted website domains are used only to perform trust analysis, calculate uptime, and improve system accuracy over time.

Cookies and Tracking

Website Trust Checker does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics tools at this time.

Data Storage

Limited technical data may be stored temporarily to support historical analysis and uptime calculations. This data is not used to identify individual users.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as the service evolves. Continued use of the website indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
